Online Conference Management

from Technology Integration Services

Welcome to the online conference management website for the University of Texas Libraries, managed by Technology Integration Services. Here you will find current and past websites for various conferences hosted at and by the University of Texas Libraries.

At present, this is a technology agnostic service, with the ability to use the CMS you are most comfortable with (Drupal and Wordpress are our recommendations), or plain HTML if you prefer. Once the event has passed, the final updates have been made and the last presentation file is uploaded, we will convert the site to “flat” HTML for archival purposes.
