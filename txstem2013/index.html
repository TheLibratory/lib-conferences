Welcome!

The 2013 Texas STEM Librarians' Conference was held at the University of Texas at Austin on Friday, July 26, 2013.  Librarians and information specialists in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and medicine came from around the state and region to share ideas and current research about all aspects of information services to students and researchers in the sci-tech fields. .  The conference was hosted by the University of Texas Libraries and was held in the University Federal Credit Union Student Learning Commons in the Perry-Castañeda Library.   

Slides and related materials from selected presentations are now available.  Click on the Proposals tab above, select a presentation, and the file link(s) (if any) will appear above the abstract.  (Some of the slideshow PDF files are quite large.)

We also took a few photographs of the conference in action:  check them out on our UT Libraries Flickr site.
